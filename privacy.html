Privacy Policy

A Basket Remains - Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

A Basket Remains is a single-player puzzle game. The game does not require an account and does not ask you to submit your name, phone number, email address, or other direct personal contact information during normal play.

Information We Process

  • Gameplay progress, level completion, stars, settings, and similar game state may be stored locally on your device.
  • The app may use the device vendor identifier (IDFV) for app security, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and service integrity.
  • If you allow App Tracking Transparency permission, advertising partners may use the advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized advertising and measurement. If you do not allow tracking, personalized tracking based on IDFA is not used.
  • Basic technical data such as app version, device type, crash information, and ad delivery status may be processed to keep the app working reliably.

Advertising

The game may show rewarded ads after eligible level completions. Advertising is initialized only after the app checks its remote ad configuration. Supported ad providers may include Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, and InMobi when valid configuration is available for the installed app bundle.

Security and Service Configuration

The app may contact a configuration service to retrieve ad unit settings for the running bundle identifier. The app may also initialize a security service using the bundle identifier and IDFV to protect the service from abuse and invalid traffic.

Children

A Basket Remains is designed to be friendly for a broad audience, but it is not directed to children under the age required by local law for consent-based data processing. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will review the request.

Your Choices

  • You can manage App Tracking Transparency permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored game progress from your device.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy questions or request assistance.
